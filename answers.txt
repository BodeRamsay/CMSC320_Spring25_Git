
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git allows you to see a full version history of your code and collaborate with other programmers

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Journalism since reporters constantly use data and statistics in their stories

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines the changes from one branch into another while preserving the commit history while Rebasing rewrites the commit history
while rebasing rewrites the commit history by moving commits from one branch on top of another branch.
